About Me

Hello! My name is Davis Troxler and I am currently studying Full-Stack Web Development at The Coding Boot Camp at UNC-Chapel Hill. I live with my wife (and our cat) in Raleigh, NC. I have been in the Triangle for about 8 years since beginning my undergraduate studies at UNC-Chapel Hill in 2010 and we both would like to plant our lives here for a long time. I am skilled in problem-solving and decided that Web Development is the perfect career for me as I thoroughly enjoy the work and building applications for customers. I desire to learn many programming languages as to further pursue my passion of Web Development.


Besides developing, I enjoy college and professional sports, traveling to new places with my wife, Motown music, and experiencing new restaurants in the Triangle. I hope you enjoy my website and contact me if you have any questions. Thank you!
